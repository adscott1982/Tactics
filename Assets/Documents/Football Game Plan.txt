Football Game
*************

Top down 2D game. Player plans the movements of individual members of football team in either attacking or defending scenarios.

Player will choose actions for all available players, and then advance a certain amount, and do so again.

In the long run, it will be part management game, with the player taking control of matches, and player development.

Actions a player can perform
****************************

On Ball:
Pass - ground pass, lofted pass
Shoot - Power shot, placed shot, chip
Dribble - Normal, sprint
Shield/Wait

Off Ball:
Run - Normal, sprint
